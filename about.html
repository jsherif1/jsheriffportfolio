John Sheriff

I'm John! I'm currently wrapping up my senior year at ASU, majoring in GIT Full Stack. My graduation can't come soon enough this December! Life is pretty full these days. I'm a husband and father to an amazing 15-month-old little girl, and we've even started dabbling in farming out here in western NY. To keep the lights on (and fund my coffee addiction!), I work as a manager at a well-known coffee chain. My path to web development has been anything but typical. Before diving into the world of code, I served 8 years in the Army Infantry. It was an intense experience that taught me a lot about discipline, teamwork, and seeing things through. I'm also a proud member of the Cheyenne River Sioux tribe, and my heritage is a huge influence on how I approach design. I believe everyone deserves access to technology, and I'm passionate about creating websites that are both inclusive and user-friendly.

More About Me

Beyond the professional stuff, there are a few other things that make me, well, me. I was adopted when I was 8, and recently reconnected with my birth family - it's been an incredible journey. When I'm not chasing after my daughter, you can usually find me with my headphones on, camera in hand, or controller in front of the screen. Music, photography, and video games are my go-to ways to unwind.
